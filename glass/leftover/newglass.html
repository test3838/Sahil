Lacquered Glass


JSR lacquered glass has a unique coloured opaque appearance. It is manufactured using special, high quality paint on one side of the glass. This give it a uniform fell and make it highly resistant to heat ,thus ensuring a lasting impression.

Why choose Lacquered glass

  • Durable :Lacquering ensures long lasting , beautiful colours
  • Easy to Use and Maintain
  • Environment-Friendly Paints Used
  • Processing:Can be Cut,Drilled,Grounded
