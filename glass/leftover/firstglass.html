Lacquered Glass

JSR lacquered glass has a unique coloured opaque appearance. It is manufactured using special, high quality paint on one side of the glass. This give it a uniform fell and make it highly resistant to heat ,thus ensuring a lasting impression.

Why choose Lacquered glass  Durable :Lacquering ensures long lasting , beautiful colours


 Easy to use ...  Easy to use and maintain

 Environment- friendly paints

 Processing: Can be cut, drilled, ground, beveled and edge-finished or polished

Features



 UV resistance prevent discolouration

 Moisture-Resistance for use in Bathrooms and Kitchen

 Opaque Look

Application

Lacquered Glass is ideal for use in domestic and commercial locations which include offices,

hotels, museums, theatres etc. for the purpose of:

• Wall paneling and column cladding

• Furniture

• Cabinet, wardrobe and sliders
• Partitions, lift lobbies
• Signage, writing boards and bathrooms.

1) Durable
2) Easy to use and maintain
3) Shiny and opaque look
