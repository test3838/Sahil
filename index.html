JSR glass studio is a glass dealer and consultant. We specialize in consultancy, Glass solutions, fabrication and installation of glass curtain walls for commercial buildings, retail malls, door and windows for projects in Delhi NCR. JSR glass studio has over 35 years of glass dealership and consultant experience, with highly trained staff. Our experienced team excels at handling the most challenging glass and mirror work.

Our glasswork is customized based on our clients choice of texture, color, thickness, size, and design.

At JSR Glass, we been helping designers and homeowners all across India with customized glass panels, shower enclosure, sinks, bathtubs and other functional glass art for years.

Whether bent, curved, cast, sandblasted, or stained glass our artists combine colors and textures to create glass master pieces that will enhance your walls, rooms, counters and floors in an intriguing and visually exciting style.
